KERNOS — Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 1, 2026

What KERNOS Is

KERNOS is a personal assistant service that you interact with via SMS and other messaging platforms. When you text the KERNOS phone number, your messages are processed by our system to provide helpful responses and manage tasks on your behalf.

What Data We Collect

When you use KERNOS, we collect:

  • Phone number — to identify your account and deliver responses.
  • Message content — the text messages you send to and receive from KERNOS.
  • Connected service data — if you connect services like Google Calendar, we access only the data needed to fulfill your requests (e.g., calendar events).
  • Usage metadata — timestamps and platform information for service operation.

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to operate the KERNOS service for you:

  • Processing and responding to your messages.
  • Maintaining conversation context so the assistant remembers prior interactions.
  • Connecting to third-party services you explicitly authorize (e.g., Google Calendar).
  • Improving the quality of responses provided to you.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

Your data may be shared only in the following limited circumstances:

  • AI processing — Message content is sent to AI language model providers (Anthropic) to generate responses. These providers process data per their own privacy policies and do not retain your data for training.
  • Messaging delivery — Your phone number and message content are transmitted through our messaging provider (Twilio) to deliver SMS messages.
  • Connected services — When you authorize a service (e.g., Google Calendar), data is exchanged with that service to fulfill your requests.
  • Legal requirements — If required by law, regulation, or legal process.

Data Retention and Deletion

Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. KERNOS uses a non-destructive deletion architecture — when data is "deleted," it is moved to an internal archive rather than permanently destroyed. You may request permanent deletion of your data by contacting us, subject to a 30-day grace period.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data, including encryption in transit and at rest. Your data is isolated per account and is not accessible to other users.

Your Rights

You may request to view, export, or permanently delete your data at any time by contacting us at the address below.
